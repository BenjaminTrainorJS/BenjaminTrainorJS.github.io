sprint three - core: emotional intelligence

24 April 2020

What is Emotional Intelligence?

  • How to interpret ourselves, where our emotions arise from, how our childhoods influence us, how we might best navigate our fears and wishes.
  • Self awareness, self management of emotions (in effective ways), social-awareness or applied empathy, social-management.
  • Self-Awareness: Knowing why I want to become a programmer. A true and clear inner sense of awareness of my true skills, interests and characteristics which act as the foundation for my journey.
  • Social-Awareness: Acknowledging how my code directly adds value to a specific group of people. Plus, the 'active listening' skills required to absorb clarity on your end user, team, and client needs.
  • You absolutely have to train your brain to be resilient.
  • Limbic System vs Neocortex

How is it different to IQ?

There are many different forms of IQ, from analytical, to commercial, but EQ is about understanding yourself.. your emotions, managing them, observing the emotional being of others and how you can help them. EQ is being aware of the present, and consciously deciding how we use our emotions for our progression and learning.

Why is it important?

It would improve the cohesion and synergy experienced within a group working environment. It would help with our self-learning, and professional development. Help us to process and work through personal and professional trauma.
